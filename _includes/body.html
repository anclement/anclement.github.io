FSL

FSL is an in-depth weekend retreat devoted to leadership development through learning and application of values within individuals and organizations at UTC. Individuals will gain a foundation for making ethical decisions, an appreciation for their organization's purpose or mission, improved interpersonal and communication skills, and an action plan fostering the growth and health of their organization and fellow members.
